Encora Apps Landing Page

Web Design

Project alongside Encora Labs

The team of Software Dev Tools is facing a transition to Encora Apps as their parent company, Nearsoft is going global as Encora, including that the subdivision is having exponential growth, having a website that expressed thos events is crutial.

Definition

Communicating everything Encora Apps was about was not easy, and required having constant communication with the team members, to find the best approach.

Process

Different design methodologies were applied to this project: User Interviews and User Journey Maps as the main tools, and also iterating to find the best structure for the landing page that fits the user's journey proposed.

Final Product

The final design involved a single page that featured the highlights of the team, including a brand style-guide that worked as a guide for future developments.
