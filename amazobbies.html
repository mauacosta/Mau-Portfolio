Amazon Hobbies

Universy-Industry Collaboration Project

"Semestre i" is a type of academic term that combines university courses with an industry project. I, with a group of selected students nationwide, worked alongside the Amazon team to explore a project that improved the shopping experience for clients.

Definition

To begin, we were given a brief that included ideas for improving the overall customer experience on Amazon, and the entire team defined the key features. These elements resulted in the final digital product: Amazon Hobbies.

Process

I led a team that worked on the design and development of the user interface.¬† We created a friendly environment for users to navigate posts created by experts using Angular as the main, but not only, front-end technology.

Final Product

The end product was delivered at a ceremony with directives from Tec de Monterrey and the Amazon Team. The web app was praised and given the highest marks by all the developing team.
